what is Aaromi

Aaromi is a managed services and portal organization in the area of people functions. With the intent of digitizing these operational activities, Aaromi's recruitment and application tracking AI portal is serving its B2B and B2C customers in the following ways: recruitment & placement services, staffing solutions, payroll, learning & development services, and many more.
